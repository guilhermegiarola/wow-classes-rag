# Havoc Demon Hunter Guide

## CLASS OVERVIEW

### Class/Spec Name and Role
- **Class/Spec**: Havoc Demon Hunter
- **Role**: DPS (Damage Per Second)

### Strengths and Weaknesses
**Strengths**:
- Powerful burst cooldowns adaptable to various encounters.
- Exceptional cleave potential due to ability enhancements.
- High mobility allows for instant repositioning.
- Strong self-sustain and defensive abilities.
- Access to crowd control options and group utility.
- Provides **Chaos Brand** debuff to assist the group.

**Weaknesses**:
- Damage is heavily frontloaded, risking threat in AoE.
- AoE abilities are reliant on cooldowns, necessitating careful planning.
- Movement-based talents can create dangerous situations.
- Optimal builds require juggling various buff windows.

### When to Play This Spec
- Best utilized in situations where high mobility and burst damage are required.
- Effective for both raids and Mythic+ dungeons.

### Playstyle Description
Havoc Demon Hunters operate on a **build-and-spend Fury** resource loop, with a focus on executing powerful burst AoEs and targeted damage. The gameplay is fast-paced, with an emphasis on clever management of cooldowns and high mobility through abilities like **Fel Rush** and **Vengeful Retreat**.

## ROTATION & ABILITIES

### Complete Priority Rotation
1. Cast **Death Sweep** during **Essence Break** debuff.
2. Cast **Annihilation** during **Essence Break** debuff.
3. Cast **Death Sweep** with **Demonsurge** active (ideally before **Metamorphosis** expires).
4. Cast **Vengeful Retreat** to trigger **Initiative**, **Inertia**, and **Exergy**.
5. Cast **Felblade** with **Inertia** ready.
6. Cast **Immolation Aura/Consuming Fire** if not currently active.
7. Keep **Immolation Aura** refreshed and manage charges.
8. Cast **Essence Break** with **Metamorphosis** active.
9. Cast **Annihilation/Chaos Strike** with **Immolation Aura** active.
10. Continue with **The Hunt**, **Death Sweep**, **Blade Dance**, etc., as outlined in the single-target or AoE rotation.

### Exact Cooldown Usage and Timing
- **Metamorphosis**: 3-minute cooldown (reduced to 2 minutes with **Rush of Chaos**)
- **Eye Beam**: 40-second cooldown.
- **Essence Break**: Critical burst window ability.
- **The Hunt**: 1.5-minute cooldown; used to generate **Reaver's Glaive**.
- **Immolation Aura**: Keep active and manage cooldowns to avoid wasting charges.

### Single Target Rotation (Detailed Step-by-Step)
1. Cast **Immolation Aura** to start.
2. Begin with **Death Sweep** during **Essence Break**.
3. Use **Felblade** to generate Fury, refreshing **Inertia**.
4. Maintain **Immolation Aura** uptime.
5. Activate **Metamorphosis** and immediately cast **Chaos Strike**.
6. Alternate between **Death Sweep** and **Annihilation** based on **Demonsurge**.
7. Use **Eye Beam** and **The Hunt** strategically.
8. Finish with fillers like **Throw Glaive** or **Fel Rush**.

### AoE/Cleave Rotation (Detailed Step-by-Step)
1. Cast **Immolation Aura** to initiate.
2. Start with **Death Sweep** during **Essence Break**.
3. Use **Fel Rush/Felblade** with **Inertia** ready.
4. Activate **Metamorphosis** and **Eye Beam** for burst.
5. Follow up with **Blade Dance** and strategic **Sigils**.
6. Continue to recast abilities while ensuring **Immolation Aura** is active.
7. Maintain **Reaver's Glaive** proc and related buffs.

### Opening Rotation/Opener Sequence
1. Cast **Immolation Aura** 2 seconds before the pull.
2. Cast **Sigil of Flame** 1 second before the pull.
3. Cast **The Hunt** to initiate.
4. Use **Felblade** to generate Fury.
5. Activate **Eye Beam** as part of your burst sequence.
6. Sequentially cast abilities ensuring **Essence Break** is aligned with **Metamorphosis**.

### Key Abilities with Effects, Cooldowns, and Resource Costs
- **Blade Dance**: Major damage ability with a cooldown; benefits from buffs like **Fury of the Aldrachi**.
- **Chaos Strike**: Base single-target attack; generates Fury.
- **Immolation Aura**: Continuous damage over time, requires careful management of charges.
- **Eye Beam**: Powerful AoE channel that resets on **Metamorphosis**.
- **Metamorphosis**: Major transformation ability granting higher damage output for its duration.

### Ability Interactions and Synergies
- **Essence Break** should be aligned with **Metamorphosis** to maximize burst damage during empowered casts.
- Maintain **Demonsurge** buffs by managing casts of abilities like **Chaos Strike** and **Death Sweep**.
- **The Hunt** generates necessary resources and buffs at crucial moments.

### Rotation Conditionals (When to Use What)
- Use **Vengeful Retreat** when needing to trigger **Initiative** and reposition quickly.
- Prioritize **Annihilation** casts when **Immolation Aura** is active for bonus damage.
- Use **Felblade** to absorb **Soul Fragments** and trigger **Reaver's Glaive**.

### Resource Management
- **Fury** is the primary resource: aim to maintain between 60-80 Fury.
- Manage **Demon Blades** to ensure efficient generation and consumption of Fury.
- Use **Felblade** under 60 Fury to keep up resource generation.

## ADDITIONAL INFO

### Tips and Tricks
- Keep an eye on **Immolation Aura** charges to prevent wasting powerful abilities.
- Align **Death Sweep** with **Essence Break** for maximum burst.
- Manage cooldowns carefully to exploit the fast-paced playstyle effectively.

### Common Mistakes
- Allowing **Immolation Aura** to sit at 2 charges can lead to significant DPS loss.
- Overcapping Fury can waste potential damage; always spend efficiently.
- Forgetting to activate abilities like **Metamorphosis** when available can lead to missed burst windows.

### Macros
- Consider creating macros for quick access to **Essence Break** followed by **Death Sweep** and **Annihilation** to streamline rotation.

### Addons
- Use addons like **WeakAuras** to track cooldowns, buffs, and charges effectively.

### Any Other Relevant Information
- Regularly sim your character to refine builds and understand the implications of gear choices.
- Familiarize yourself with rotations through practice and by using training dummies to hone your timing and sequencing.
