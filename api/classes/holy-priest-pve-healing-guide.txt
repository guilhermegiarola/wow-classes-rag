# Holy Priest Comprehensive Guide

## 1. CLASS OVERVIEW

### Class/Spec Name and Role
- **Class/Spec:** Holy Priest
- **Role:** Healer

### Strengths and Weaknesses
- **Strengths:**
  - Excels at reactive raid healing, particularly in single-target triage.
  - Unique short-cooldown healing style providing regular bursts of healing.
  - Powerful raid cooldowns.
  - Good single target and AoE damage potential, but only when not healing.
  
- **Weaknesses:**
  - Many abilities to track.
  - Low AoE burst healing throughput.
  - Poor damage while healing compared to most other healers.
  - No interrupt ability.

### When to Play This Spec
- Ideal for groups or encounters where reactive healing is paramount, and significant damage is expected.
- Great choice for players who enjoy strategic healing mechanics and ability management.

### Playstyle Description
- Holy Priests are reactive healers focused on responding to incoming damage with specific healing spells and cooldowns. They utilize a combination of short-cooldown abilities to maximize healing output efficiently while managing resources like Mana.

## 2. ROTATION & ABILITIES

### Complete Priority Rotation
1. Lightwell near allies, usually between melee and ranged.
2. Divine Word followed by Holy Word: Sanctify (if available) on a cluster of injured allies.
3. Prayer of Mending when you have Premonition of Insight.
4. Halo immediately before, or more than 60 seconds before Apotheosis.
5. Apotheosis with 0 or 1 charge of each Holy Word.
6. Holy Word: Sanctify when a cluster of your raid is injured.
7. Holy Word: Serenity on an injured ally.
8. Holy Word: Chastise to trigger Divine Image.
9. Flash Heal with 1 or more stacks of Surge of Light.
10. Power Word: Life for emergency triage on an ally near death.
11. Divine Star when most of your raid is injured.
12. Prayer of Mending on cooldown.
13. Renew on a tank or debuff target.
14. Holy Nova near 5 or more injured allies at 16+ stacks of Rhapsody.
15. Flash Heal for healing one ally.
16. Flash Heal when affected by Surge of Light.
17. Prayer of Healing for general purpose healing.
18. Prayer of Healing with 1 or more stacks of Lightweaver for healing multiple allies.
19. Heal with 1 or more stacks of Lightweaver for healing one ally.
20. Heal with 1 or more stacks of Lightweaver for general purpose healing.
21. Flash Heal with 0 stacks of Lightweaver.
22. Smite an enemy when no healing is required.

### Exact Cooldown Usage and Timing
- **Apotheosis:** Use as often as possible, aligns with optimized Holy Word use.
- **Divine Hymn:** Utilize during heavy raid damage phases.
- **Halo:** Coordinate with major cooldowns for maximum effect.

### Single Target Rotation
- Focus on using **Holy Word: Serenity**, filling with **Flash Heal** (with **Surge of Light** procs), and maintain **Renew** on targets who need it.

### AoE/Cleave Rotation
- Begin with **Lightwell** for general healing, follow with **Divine Word** and **Holy Word: Sanctify**, casting **Halo** when feasible. Use **Prayer of Mending** and maximize **Holy Nova** during AoE situations.

### Opening Rotation/Opener Sequence
1. Lightwell placement.
2. Divine Word and Holy Word: Sanctify.
3. Immediately apply Prayer of Mending where appropriate.

### Key Abilities with Effects, Cooldowns, and Resource Costs
| Ability                   | Effect                                                       | Cooldown                | Resource Cost         |
|---------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------|-------------------------|------------------------|
| **Holy Word: Serenity**   | Strong single-target heal                                    | 30 sec                  | None                   |
| **Holy Word: Sanctify**   | AoE heal                                                    | 30 sec                  | None                   |
| **Divine Hymn**           | Channeled AoE healing                                       | 3 min                   | None                   |
| **Apotheosis**            | Increases healing output, reduces Holy Word cooldowns       | 3 min                   | None                   |
| **Flash Heal**            | Quick heal to target, generates Lightweaver stacks         | 6 sec cooldown          | Low Mana               |
| **Prayer of Mending**     | Bounces between allies, providing healing                   | None                    | Moderate Mana          |
| **Renew**                 | HoT effect on target                                        | 12 sec                  | Low Mana               |
| **Lightwell**             | Placeable heal over time for allies within proximity        | 3 min                   | Moderate Mana          |

### Ability Interactions and Synergies
- **Surge of Light:** Procced by Flash Heal and Smite, allowing for reduced cooldown on Holy Words.
- **Apotheosis** enhances cooldown reduction for Holy Words, facilitating more frequent cast cycles.
- **Lightweaver** stacks are best spent on Prayer of Healing or Heal for enhanced efficiency.

### Rotation Conditionals
- Use **Flash Heal** after proccing **Surge of Light**.
- When using **Apotheosis**, prioritize **Holy Word** casts to reduce their cooldowns quickly.

### Resource Management (Runes, Runic Power, etc.)
- Focus on maintaining Mana through proper cooldown usage, especially with **Shadowfiend** returning resources during high damage phases.

## 3. ADDITIONAL INFO

### Tips and Tricks
- Manage **Lightwell** positioning to maximize ally access during movement phases.
- Keep track of stacking **Premonition** and try to align it with major cooldowns for maximum benefit.

### Common Mistakes
- Overhealing due to mismanagement of cooldowns; casting spells without considering the likely overheal can waste resources.
- Failing to utilize **Prayer of Mending** at optimal times can reduce overall healing effectiveness.

### Macros
- Suggest using macro combinations for **Prayer of Mending** and major cooldowns for quick selection during high-pressure times.
  
### Addons
- Utilize **WeakAuras** to track cooldowns, especially for **Prayer of Mending** and **Surge of Light**.
- **Grid** or **VuhDo** can help in monitoring group health effectively.

### Any Other Relevant Information
- For Mythic+ dungeons, adopting aggressive playstyle early with cooldowns allows for better group survival through heavy hits.
- **Guardian Spirit** is invaluable; using it correctly can save lives in critical moments, particularly for tanks.
