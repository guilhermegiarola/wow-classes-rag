## Brewmaster Monk Class Overview

### Class/Spec Name and Role
- Brewmaster Monk (Tank)

### Strengths and Weaknesses
- **Strengths:**
  - Strong damage intake management via **Stagger**, **Shuffle**, and **Mastery: Elusive Brawler**.
  - High mobility with abilities like **Roll**, **Tiger's Lust**, and **Transcendence**.
  - Flexibility in playstyle and mitigation strategies.
  - Significant utility for Mythic+, such as **Detox**, **Paralysis**, and **Leg Sweep**.
  - Unique **Mystic Touch** debuff for increased damage taken by targets.

- **Weaknesses:**
  - Low base health relative to other tank specializations.
  - Requires many keybinds for optimal performance.
  - Limited long-term self-sustain outside of **Expel Harm** and **Celestial Fortune**.
  - Can be unintuitive in playstyle, leading to risk of mistakes.
  - Often requires addons or WeakAuras for tracking **Stagger** effectively.

### When to Play this Spec
- Ideal for players who enjoy a blend of tanking and damage dealing.
- A strong choice for both progression raiding and Mythic+ dungeons due to survivability and utility.
  
### Playstyle Description
- Brewmaster Monks focus on managing incoming damage while maintaining high mobility. They utilize a mix of damage-dealing abilities to generate **Stagger** effects, dodging attacks to reduce damage taken. This specialization demands awareness of cooldown management and timing to maximize damage reduction and survival.

---

## Rotation & Abilities

### Complete Priority Rotation
1. Activate **Rushing Jade Wind** before combat.
2. Use **Chi Burst** while moving to the target.
3. Use **Keg Smash** on approach.
4. Use **Blackout Kick**.
5. Use **Tiger Palm**.
6. Use **Breath of Fire**.
7. Use **Rising Sun Kick** to trigger **Chi Wave**.
8. Activate **Weapons of Order**.
9. Use **Keg Smash**.
10. Cycle through **Blackout Kick**, **Tiger Palm**, and **Rising Sun Kick** as able.

### Exact Cooldown Usage and Timing
- Maintain a rhythm by utilizing **Blackout Kick** every 4 seconds as it has a fixed cooldown, while managing **Purifying Brew**, **Celestial Brew**, and others as they come off cooldown. Use **Expel Harm** when healing spheres from **Gift of the Ox** are available.

### Single Target Rotation
1. Activate **Black Ox Brew** for energy restoration if low.
2. Use **Touch of Death** when applicable.
3. Use **Rising Sun Kick** at 10 stacks of **Press the Advantage**.
4. Use **Blackout Kick** to trigger **Blackout Combo**.
5. Use **Chi Burst**, prioritizing to maintain stacks of **Balanced Stratagem**.
6. Continue using **Keg Smash**, **Rushing Jade Wind**, and **Spinning Crane Kick** based on resource availability and damage situations.

### AoE/Cleave Rotation
- Similar to the single target but prioritize hitting multiple mobs and maintain **Rushing Jade Wind** for damage. Utilize **Exploding Keg** cleverly to increase AoE damage and apply **Breath of Fire** and **Blackout Kick** at the right moments.

### Opening Rotation/Opener Sequence
- Same as above, ensuring you build setup to **Shuffle** for damage mitigation immediately at the start of a fight.

### Key Abilities
- **Keg Smash:** Main damage ability; procs **Shuffle** and interrupts.
- **Blackout Kick:** Rotational ability that can trigger **Blackout Combo**.
- **Breath of Fire:** Applies a debuff; strong in AoE situations.
- **Rising Sun Kick:** Deals damage and triggers **Chi Wave**.
- **Purifying Brew:** Removes **Staggered** damage; essential for survival.
- **Celestial Brew/Celestial Infusion:** Absorbs damage based on missing health.
  
### Ability Interactions and Synergies
- **Stagger:** Damage mitigation mechanic that splits damage into immediate and DoT effects.
- **Shuffle:** Enhances **Stagger**, requiring activation through **Blackout Kick** or **Keg Smash**.
- ***Weapons of Order:** Provided burst damage boost and resets **Keg Smash** cooldown.
  
### Rotation Conditionals
- Prioritize defensive abilities when health drops low.
- Use **Economic Charge** from abilities wisely, ensuring you bank resets effectively.
  
### Resource Management
- Monitor **Energy**, **Stagger**, and cooldowns to maximize damage output and survivability.

---

## Additional Info

### Tips and Tricks
- Keep **Shuffle** active through careful coordination between abilities, especially following **Keg Smash**.
- Use **Purifying Brew** effectively to manage your **Stagger** damage.

### Common Mistakes
- Forgetting to maintain **Shuffle** or **Stagger** leading to sudden spikes in damage received.
- Irregular usage of **Purifying Brew**, causing loss of potential healing.

### Macros
- Suggested macros for abilities like **Touch of Death** and **Rushing Jade Wind** to streamline gameplay.

### Addons
- Utilize addons like **WeakAuras** for tracking **Stagger** and **Healing Sphere** counts effectively.

### Other Relevant Information
- **Invoke Niuzao, the Black Ox:** Active ability that transfers **Stagger** damage and helps mitigate incoming damage while adding damage output.
- **Niuzao's Resolve:** Changes the healing properties of **Gift of the Ox** to a HoT, providing steady healing over time rather than bursts.

---

### Note on Patch Changes
- Most strategies and abilities are adapted to version **11.2.5**, and can be altered in future patches.
