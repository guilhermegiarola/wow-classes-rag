## Windwalker Monk Guide

### 1. CLASS OVERVIEW

- **Class/Spec Name and Role**: Windwalker Monk - Melee DPS
- **Strengths and Weaknesses**:
  - **Strengths**:
    - High mobility
    - Strong presence in Mythic+ dungeons
    - Good burst damage during cooldown windows
    - Can easily switch between single-target and AoE damage
    - Varied talent choices for flexibility in playstyle
  - **Weaknesses**:
    - Historically lower DPS output compared to other classes in raids
    - No unique immunities or utility abilities
    - "Desirability" fluctuates based on the strength of Mistweaver and Brewmaster specs
- **When to Play This Spec**: Optimal in Mythic+ and situations requiring strong mobility and switch targeting.
- **Playstyle Description**: Windwalker Monks manage Energy and Chi to optimize a rotation of high-impact abilities. The gameplay focuses on fast-paced, dynamic interactions with a balance between damage dealing and resource management.

#### Key Abilities with Effects, Cooldowns, and Resource Costs

- **Tiger Palm**: Generates 1 Chi; used to prevent Energy overflow.
- **Rising Sun Kick**: Major Chi spender with high damage; cooldown 8 seconds.
- **Fists of Fury**: AoE ability; high damage; cooldown 30 seconds.
- **Invoke Xuen, the White Tiger**: Summons a tiger to attack enemies; significant damage boost; cooldown 3 minutes.
- **Spinning Crane Kick**: AoE ability that can cost Chi depending on a proc; important while talented into Dance of Chi-Ji.
- **Celestial Conduit**: Buffs ability damage; enhances impact during Heart of the Jade Serpent.
  
#### Ability Interactions and Synergies

- **Blackout Kick**: Interacts with Mastery: Combo Strikes; try to use while managing the Chi buff.
- **Dance of Chi-Ji**: Increases effectiveness of Spinning Crane Kick; requires proactive use to maximize DPS.
  
#### Rotation Conditionals

- Use **Fists of Fury** as soon as it is available if you have enough Haste.
- Prioritize **Rising Sun Kick** when the Xuen aura is active.
- Monitor and avoid capping on Chi and Energy during high-damage cooldowns.

#### Resource Management

- **Chi Management**: Key to avoid capping on either Chi or Energy to maximize damage output.
- Windwalkers will generate excess resources during cooldown windows; strategic use of abilities can prevent waste.

### 3. ADDITIONAL INFO

#### Tips and Tricks

- Practice your rotation without cooldowns to master the basic skill flow.
- Stay aware of cooldown timings and adjust your priorities based on buffs and procs active.
- Use **Combat Assistant** for simplified rotations when learning the spec.

#### Common Mistakes

- Overcapping Energy and Chi during cooldowns.
- Ignoring procs like Dance of Chi-Ji, leading to lost damage potential.
- Failing to react promptly to proc opportunities for maximizing output.

#### Macros

- Recommended to use macros for abilities to avoid misclicks and reduce response time during raids/dungeons. Check the Addons page for examples.

#### Addons

- Tracking buffs, procs, and cooldowns can be simplified with specific addons (e.g., WeakAuras).
- Look for DPS meters to analyze your performance and optimize your rotation further.

#### Other Relevant Information

- Windwalker Monks require practice to master due to their complex rotation and the need to react to various situations in combat effectively.
- The spec remains competitive in Mythic+ dungeons, especially with the right talents and cooldown management.
