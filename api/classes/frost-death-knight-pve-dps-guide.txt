## Frost Death Knight Guide

### 1. CLASS OVERVIEW

**Class/spec name and role:**  
Frost Death Knight - Melee DPS

**Strengths and weaknesses:**
- **Strengths:**  
  - Good single target with a 45 seconds burst profile
  - Naturally strong cleave
  - Great add control with Death Grip
  - Knockback prevention with Death's Advance
  - Immunity to many magical debuffs via Anti-Magic Shell
  - Anti-Magic Zone as a magic damage raid cooldown
  - One of the tankiest specs
- **Weaknesses:**  
  - Limited spread add cleave
  - Limited mobility
  - Susceptible to forced downtime during cooldowns
  - Doesn't provide a very powerful raid buff
  - Limited Mythic+ utility

**When to play this spec:**  
Play as a Frost Death Knight when looking to deal significant direct damage, especially in raid encounters and Mythic+ dungeons where you can take advantage of cleave.

**Playstyle description:**  
Frost Death Knight gameplay revolves around managing resources effectively, maximizing burst damage within cooldown windows, and ensuring proper positioning to optimize AoE damage.

### 2. ROTATION & ABILITIES

#### Complete priority rotation

1. **Single Target:**  
   - Use Obliterate if you have a proc of Killing Machine.
   - Use Howling Blast if you have a proc of Rime.
   - Use Frost Strike when you do not have other abilities available.
   - Use Obliterate as a runic power dump when applicable.

2. **Cleave/Mythic+:**  
   - Use Frostscythe if you have a proc of Killing Machine.
   - Use Obliterate if you have a proc of Killing Machine.
   - Use Howling Blast if you have a proc of Rime.
   - Use Remorseless Winter on cooldown.
   - Use Frost Strike if you have a proc of Frostbane.
   - Use Glacial Advance.
   - Use Frostscythe.
   - Use Obliterate.

#### Exact cooldown usage and timing

- **Cooldowns:**  
  - Reaper's Mark: 45-second cooldown; align with Pillar of Frost.
  - Pillar of Frost: 45-second cooldown; grants 30% Strength for 12 seconds.
  - Remorseless Winter: Use on cooldown, ideally with Frozen Dominion or to maintain Everfrost.
  - Empower Rune Weapon: Use when close to 2 stacks to prevent overcapping.
  - Breath of Sindragosa: 60 Runic Power, lasts 8 seconds; can be extended with procs.
  - Frostwyrm's Fury: Used ideally during Pillar of Frost for maximum beneficent damage.

#### Opening rotation/opener sequence

1. **Single Target with Breath/Empower:**  
   1. Remorseless Winter  
   2. Cursed Stone Idol  
   3. Reaper's Mark + Empower Rune Weapon  
   4. Pillar of Frost + Obliterate + Breath of Sindragosa + Trinket + Potion + Raise Dead  
   5. Repeat Obliterate, Frostscythe, with emphasis on maximizing Killing Machine usage.

#### Ability interactions and synergies

- **Empower Rune Weapon** synergizes with **Obliteration** for maximum resource management by generating multiple procs while keeping rune costs minimal.
- **Breath of Sindragosa** integrated with **Pillar of Frost** and **Killing Machines** results in substantial burst damage.

#### Rotation conditionals

- **Before using Obliterate:** Ensure you have a proc of Killing Machine active.
- **If low on Runes, first consume:** Rime procs through Howling Blast then focus on Killing Machine.
- **In AoE situations:** Use Frostscythe as your primary rune spender.

#### Resource management

- **Runes:** You have a total of 6 Runes. They recharge over time; aim to keep 3 Runes on cooldown.
- **Runic Power:** Maximum of 100. Generated primarily through consuming Runes and certain abilities.

### 3. ADDITIONAL INFO

#### Tips and tricks
- **Positioning matters:** Stand at the edge of enemy packs to maximize damage from frontal AoE abilities.
- **Control your cooldowns:** Utilizing macros can help maximize GCD usage with abilities such as Pillar of Frost.

#### Common mistakes
- Neglecting to consume Killing Machine procs promptly.
- Overcapping Runic Power or Runes due to improper timing.

#### Macros
- Recommended to use combined macros for Pillar of Frost along with other abilities to ensure optimal casting while minimizing downtime.

#### Addons
- Combat Assistants can aid in rotation execution, though manual usage of major cooldowns will yield higher DPS.
