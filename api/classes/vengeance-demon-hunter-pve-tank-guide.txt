## CLASS OVERVIEW

### Class/Spec Name and Role
- **Class/Spec:** Vengeance Demon Hunter
- **Role:** PvE Tank

### Strengths and Weaknesses
- **Strengths:**
  - High mobility
  - Burst AoE damage capabilities
  - Access to strong defensive tools
  - Strong crowd control options
  - Good magic damage mitigation
  - Provides group utility with **Chaos Brand**
  
- **Weaknesses:**
  - Struggles defensively at the start of combat
  - Lower single-target damage compared to other tanks
  - Mitigation and healing suffers from rotational mistakes, particularly in the **Aldrachi Reaver** build

### When to Play This Spec
- Best suited for players who enjoy a mobile tanking style that emphasizes burst damage, crowd control, and high survivability.

### Playstyle Description
- Vengeance Demon Hunters focus on resource management, utilizing **Soul Fragments** for healing while managing high mobility to navigate fights. Their style involves building up resources rapidly and utilizing high-impact abilities efficiently.

---

## ROTATION & ABILITIES

### Complete Priority Rotation

1. **Use Reaver's Glaive** when there is 4 seconds left on **Thrill of the Fight** or if it is not active.
2. **Use Fracture** buffed by **Art of the Glaive** as the first ability in the combo.
3. **Use Shear** buffed by **Art of the Glaive**.
4. **Use Soul Cleave** buffed by **Art of the Glaive**.
5. Use other abilities according to conditional priorities until capped on resources.

### Exact Cooldown Usage and Timing
- **Metamorphosis:** Increases health by 40% and armor by 200% for 15 seconds. Use proactively.
- **Fiery Brand:** Defensive cooldown; apply when anticipating incoming damage.
- **Demon Spikes:** Active mitigation ability; use frequently to reduce physical damage taken.
- **Infernal Strike:** Off-global cooldown ability for additional damage, manage for movement.

### Single Target Rotation (Detailed Step-by-Step)
1. **Pre-cast Immolation Aura** 2 seconds before pull.
2. **Pre-place Sigil of Flame** 1 second before pull.
3. **Use Fiery Brand**
4. **Use Felblade**
5. **Use The Hunt**
6. **Use Fel Devastation**
7. **Use Spirit Burst**
8. **Use Soul Sunder**
9. **Use Sigil of Flame**
10. **Use Metamorphosis** before Sigil of Flame lands.
11. Proceed with normal Rotation according to the priority list.

### AoE/Cleave Rotation (Detailed Step-by-Step)
1. **Use Reaver's Glaive** when close to 4 seconds left on **Thrill of the Fight** or if it’s inactive.
2. **Use Fracture** buffed by **Art of the Glaive**.
3. **Use Shear** bufed by **Art of the Glaive**.
4. **Use Soul Cleave** when ample resources are available.
5. **Use The Hunt** on cooldown, applying to as many targets as possible.
6. Continue using abilities according to AoE priority listing.

### Opening Rotation/Opener Sequence
- Follow the single target opener listed above, effectively transitioning into the main rotation as you generate resources.

### Key Abilities
- **Reaver's Glaive:** Buffs damage and resources.
- **Spirit Bomb:** High burst AOE damage.
- **Soul Cleave:** Primary resource spender for healing and damage.
- **Metamorphosis:** Major cooldown enhancing damage and survival.
- **Infernal Strike:** Mobility and extra damage, essential for repositioning.

### Ability Interactions & Synergies
- **Art of the Glaive** pairs with **Fracture** and **Soul Cleave**, increasing generated resources.
- **The Hunt** is critical for usage right before heavy damage rotations to maximize its effects.
- **Fiery Brand** can amplify damage during cooldown phases.

### Rotation Conditionals (When to Use What)
- Use **Metamorphosis** immediately when entering a fight against substantial damage.
- **Fiery Brand** should be applied strategically based on predicted damage spikes.

### Resource Management
- **Soul Fragments**: Ensure they're generated primarily from **Fracture** and **Shear** to maximize healing and damage output through **Soul Bomb** and **Soul Cleave**.

## ADDITIONAL INFO

### Tips and Tricks
- Maintain mobility and position well to maximize the effectiveness of **Infernal Strike**.
- Mismanaging your resources severely impacts both damage and survivability, practice makes perfect.

### Common Mistakes
- Using **Metamorphosis** reactively instead of proactively.
- Not managing **Demon Spikes** effectively, leading to unnecessary damage taken.

### Macros
- Recommended to create macros for **Metamorphosis** to ensure it’s used optimally.
- Create a macro for utility spells that prioritize target engagement.

### Addons
- Combat Assistant: This addon simplifies abilities based on preset priorities, useful for visual guidance during combat.
- Rotational aids specific to Vengeance can enhance play efficiency.

### Other Relevant Information
- Utilize **Soul Fragments** wisely; only consume them when necessary to maintain healing and damage bonuses.
- Tailor talent builds to your specific encounter needs, adapting your playstyle to the fight mechanics.
