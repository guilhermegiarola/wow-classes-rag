## CLASS OVERVIEW

### Class/Spec Name and Role
- **Class/Spec Name**: Preservation Evoker
- **Role**: Healer

### Strengths and Weaknesses
**Strengths:**
- Extremely strong stacked healing through **Dream Breath** and **Emerald Blossom**.
- Powerful party and raid cooldowns such as **Dream Flight**, **Rewind**, and **Zephyr**.
- High mobility with **Hover**, **Verdant Embrace**, and **Rescue**.
- Fairly high damage output with **Fire Breath** and various talent options.
- Strong defensives through **Obsidian Scales**, with two charge potential from **Obsidian Bulwark**.

**Weaknesses:**
- Weak single-target healing capabilities.
- Lack of strong emergency abilities aside from **Rewind**.
- Empowered spells (e.g., **Dream Breath**, **Spiritbloom**, **Fire Breath**) have long cast times, making casting timely more difficult.
- 30-yard range on most spells, complicating healing for spread allies.

### When to Play This Spec
- Best played in stacked environments for maximizing AoE healing.
- Suitable for both raids and Mythic+ dungeons, especially in scenarios involving high raid damage.

### Playstyle Description
- The Preservation Evoker excels in using a flexible spell kit tailored to current in-game circumstances, focusing on area of effect healing and positioning to aid allies while maintaining mobility and damage output.

---

## ROTATION & ABILITIES

### Complete Priority Rotation
1. **Maintain Stasis on cooldown** to store powerful heals (preferably **Spiritbloom**, **Dream Breath**, or **Temporal Anomaly**).
2. Use **Tip the Scales** to enhance your next healing spell.
3. Generate **Echos** using **Temporal Anomaly** as mana allows.
4. Spend **Echos** on:
   - **Verdant Embrace** (high burst healing)
   - **Spiritbloom** (steady healing)
   - **Dream Breath** (for heavy DoT situations)
   - **Reversion** (if healing over time is needed).
5. Utilize **Fire Breath** for damage and Essence generation.
6. Cast **Living Flame** when not in a high-healing scenario to generate Essence Burst.
7. Avoid overcapping on Essence.

### Exact Cooldown Usage and Timing
1. **Dream Flight**: Use when damage is incoming, targeting as many allies as possible.
2. **Stasis**: Utilize to store powerful spells; only store weak spells if needed as fillers.
3. **Rewind**: Activate after heavy damage spikes; healing is based on damage taken in the previous 5 seconds.
4. **Tip the Scales**: Use in situations of unexpected damage for an instant heal.
5. **Zephyr**: Use to reduce AoE damage for up to 5 allies.

### Single Target Rotation
1. **Verdant Embrace** on the target.
2. Follow with an **Echo** on yourself.
3. Cast **Spiritbloom** on yourself for maximum transfer of healing.

### AoE/Cleave Rotation
1. Cast **Emerald Blossom** as often as possible.
2. Follow with **Temporal Anomaly** to generate Echos for area heals.
3. Use **Dream Breath** to cover larger periods of sustained damage.
4. Apply **Reversion** to maintain healing over time on multiple allies.

### Opening Rotation/Opener Sequence
1. Start with **Tip the Scales**.
2. Follow with **Stasis** to store a powerful healing spell (e.g., **Dream Breath**).
3. Use **Fire Breath** to generate essence.
4. Cast **Living Flame** to build up essence.
5. Start healing through **Verdant Embrace** and other healing abilities based on damage received.

### Ability Interactions and Synergies
- **Verdant Embrace** followed by **Echo** and **Spiritbloom** allows for massive healing through Lifebind.
- **Stasis** can be used to store spells like **Temporary Anomaly** so that you can use them effectively during a burst healing situation.

### Rotation Conditionals
- Use **Echo** primarily with **Verdant Embrace** to maximize burst healing when the raid is low on health.
- Switch to **Emerald Blossom** in high spread damage situations where multiple allies are at risk.

### Resource Management
- **Essence** is central: never cap on it and prefer to spend it wisely on the correct healing abilities.
- **Essence Burst** allows for powerful essence spending gains, particularly following spells like **Fire Breath** and **Living Flame**.

## ADDITIONAL INFO

### Tips and Tricks
- Maintain a good grip on your cooldowns to ensure maximum efficiency.
- Use **Lifebind** strategically with abilities like **Emerald Communion** for massive healing bursts.

### Common Mistakes
- Forgetting to rotate cooldowns and not optimizing healing output based on group dynamics.
- Overcapping on essence by failing to consume it with useful spells.

### Macros
- Recommended to have macros for **Emerald Blossom** and **Rewind** for quicker casting.

### Addons
- Use a healer-focused add-on that helps monitor cooldowns, and manage healing output effectively.

### Any Other Relevant Information
- Played effectively, Preservation Evokers can significantly contribute to raid healing through high mobility and strategic healing tool usage.
